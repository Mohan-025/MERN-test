Two Way Booking Discount:

1.10% discount on return journey basic fare is allowed when both onward and return journey tickets are booked in a single transaction.

2.Onward & Return Booking Discount will be provided for All Classes and All corporation buses.

3.Onward & Return booking discount is not applicable on Weekends, Festival and Auspicious Travel Days.

4.Allowed on Same Class of service for Travel on both Onward & Return.

5.At any instance “SINGLE DISCOUNT” will be applicable on valid booking tickets i.e. whichever discount is Higher.

6.Partial Cancellation Allowed.

Senior Citizens:

1.Fare concessions availed by senior citizens, have to produce original age proof ID cards at the time of journey like PAN card, Voter's ID, Driving license, Passport, Ration card, etc.

2.At the time of boarding the vehicle, photocopy of the Age Proof ID has to be handed over to the Conductor.

Cancer Patient and their Escorts:

1.Incase Cancer Patient alone travelling can avail 75% Concession and Cancer Patient with Escort travelling can avail 50% Concession.

2.Cancer Patient Concession is not Applicable in AC Sleeper, AC Sleeper Seater, Non-AC Sleeper class of Services.
